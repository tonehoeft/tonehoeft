TV & Commercial

I’ve worked on a variety of projects in broadcast, most notably as the head writer and producer of Hallmark Channel’s Hero Dog Awards for three years. I’ve also written on-air content for ESPN, Foot Locker, and Disney. See a sample of my TV and commercial work by checking out my creative reel below or visiting my IMDb page.

Editor

A great editor doesn’t just edit – they schedule, plan, and coordinate with the writing team. At Poweranks, I worked as the editor-in-chief, where I oversaw a team of 20+ writers and 3 editors to publish 3-4 stories daily, and before that, was the managing editor of the Russian Journal of Communication and editor-at-large at Toyota Motors Thailand.

Videos & Podcasts

Anyone can write for web videos and podcasts, but I can also shoot, produce, and edit them too. I’ve produced web shows and podcasts for Poweranks along with videos for various tech start-ups.


Social Media & Copy Writer

I've ventured into the world of social media and copy writing by tackling the art of the pithy and informational excerpt for Poweranks and writing the commercial scripts for all the projects in the TV & Commercial section above for clients like Disney, Foot Locker, and ESPN.
